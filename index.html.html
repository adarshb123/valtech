About Us

Valtek industries is one of the leading manufacturers of Laminated Tubes. An ISO 9001-2008 company, Valtek has a state of the art fully automatic Laminated Tube making facility at, Hubli. The Unit is being run in virtually dust free and hygienic conditions i.e (Clean Room) with the entire shop floor being air conditioned. Our team is highly experienced and is fully trained, to perform with utmost care. Valtek industries has a fully functional internal QC Lab and strict online QA procedures that insure high quality of its products. Our values drive our high performance & superior customer service.

ABL

The ABL tubes give good “collapsible” effect which is desirable where precise dispensing of the product is required. Today, ABL tubes are not only used for toothpaste applications, but also increasingly for cosmetics, food, pharmaceutical and household and industrial applications.

PBL

PBL tubes or Laminated Tubes are made completely of plastic with the barrier layer being made of Ethyl Vinyl alcohol (EVOH) plastic granules, EVOH is primarily used in applications where the packaging needs to look extra attractive, especially cosmetic products.

ABL

This is the packaging most commonly used for toothpaste throughout the world. ABL Tubes combines an economic solution with high quality printing options in a proven protective collapsible tube. ABL can be used to make packaging tubes to hold aggressive products including liniment creams, essential oils, antiseptics and adhesives.

Our ABL tubes also are available with the Tamper Evident Safety Seal(TESS) that protects the orifice of the tube from exposure prior to consumer use. A Tube Advantage ABL Tube is a proven protective packaging solution. Contact Valtek Industries for more information.
